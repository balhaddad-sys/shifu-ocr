# Shifu — Philosophical Framework

## The Governing Law

Form distinguishes. Context relates. History remembers.
Influence reshapes. Contrast identifies. Expectation directs.
Understanding arises between them.

## Core Principles

### 1. Meaning cannot emerge from nothing
The hand-authored features are the observer's encoded prior distinctions — the seed the vessel carries. Data does not create meaning from emptiness. It allows meaning-like structure to form from prior encoded distinctions.

### 2. Intelligent design is interference, not governance
The features prepare conditions. They do not force outcomes. What forms, forms under these conditions. What does not, does not.

### 3. Understanding is relational, never in isolation
A word in isolation can carry form. But understanding requires relation — to neighboring words, to prior exposures, to temporal recurrence, to contrast, to what it is not. Form can exist alone. Understanding cannot.

### 4. Understanding is measured by relational discrimination, not agreement
A formed word should relate differently to different things. High variance in similarity across relations is a proxy for understanding. Low variance — flat response to everything — indicates the word was seen but not understood.

### 5. Identity is known through contrast
A thing knows itself by what it is not. "I am alive because I am not under dirt." The engine develops a self-model (baseline expectations). Each word's identity is partly how it deviates from that baseline. Different engines develop different baselines — same word, different identity.

### 6. The engine IS the perspective
A camel is transport or food depending on the observer. The observer is the engine. What you feed it is what it becomes. What it becomes is how it sees. Meaning is perspective-dependent because the vessel was fed particular experiences, not all experiences.

### 7. The brain expects a finite set of next words
That finite set IS the directional structure. "Doctor treats ___" expects "patient." "Patient treats ___" expects differently. The gap between expectation and reality is composition. No parsing. No roles. Just: what did the engine expect, and what came?

### 8. Meaning is understood retrospectively
You do not watch it form. You look back and it is there, or it is not.

### 9. The data is what it is
Positive results are not proof. Negative results are the instrument doing its job. The value is in making the question answerable, not in getting the answer you want.
